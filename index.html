Profile / Biography

My name is Michael Celata, and my goal is to pursue a career in the Mechanical Engineering area of study.

Being a senior in Advanced Manufacturing at Essex North Shore Agricultural & Technical School has led me to gain the skills that have increased my technical ability, as well as my confidence and responsibility. Through careful planning, problem-solving, and persistence, I have successfully completed different shop projects that have made me understand the manufacturing process deeply.

I originally intended since the seventh grade to major in computer science when I went to college, however, Over the past years, I’ve been interested in all of the ways that similar critical thinking was used in engineering, such as Mechanical Engineering. New knowledge that I have accumulated throughout the Advanced Manufacturing Program and other experiences in my life have provided insight in the way products are designed with precision, leading to a more engineering-focused mindset.

I enjoy solving complex projects and paying attention to details.
