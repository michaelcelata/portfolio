MEFA Podcast

I was asked to participate in a MEFA (Massachusetts Educational Financing Authority) podcast to represent a student in the Advanced Manufacturing Program at Essex North Shore Agricultural & Technical School. My role was to be interviewed about my career area, Advanced Manufacturing. I was called down after the MEFA representatives arrived to answer questions about myself and why I chose to attend Essex North Shore Agricultural & Technical School. The MEFA representatives took photos and videos of me while I showed them what I learn and do in my career area, including hands-on projects and technical equipment used in the program such as the robot and CMM.

I was asked various questions about what I have learned in my course of study, how I imagine using these skills in the future, how I balance my coursework and program training, and how my school has helped prepare me for my future college plans. Because I have strong written and verbal communication skills, I am confident presenting material in front of various audiences. This experience has helped me become more comfortable presenting to different audiences and reinforced my interest in going to college for Mechanical Engineering after graduation.
