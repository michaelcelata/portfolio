I would like to pursue a career in the mechanical engineering area of study. Being a senior in Advanced Manufacturing at Essex North Shore Agricultural & Technical School has led me to gain the skills that have increased my confidence and responsibility. Through careful planning, problem-solving, and persistence, I have successfully completed different projects that met all required parameters and constraints. I have recently been selected to join SkillsUSA to compete in the upcoming SkillsUSA state and national levels. I originally intended, since the 7th grade, to major in computer science when I went to college. Over the past few years, I’ve been interested in all of the ways that similar critical thinking was used in engineering, such as mechanical engineering. New knowledge that I have accumulated throughout the Advanced Manufacturing program and other experiences in my life have provided insight in the way products are designed with precision, leading to a more engineering-focused mindset. I enjoy solving complex projects and paying attention to details. I realized that I can work independently or on a team using my prior skills and creativity to achieve success.
