Competition

The Skills USA state competition for Automated Manufacturing was very fun and challenging

This is an example of the FANUC control on our Levil machine we used for SkillsUSA

For the competition, I was responsible for making a 3D CAD model from a blueprint drawing, and to make a new technical drawing from the CAD. The other members of our team were involved in the CAM and setup of the part. Overall, we all helped each other and played a part in every process. We ended up being successful despite making tons of mistakes, and ranked 3rd in the US state of Massachusetts for Automated Manufacturing.
